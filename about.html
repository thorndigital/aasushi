Our Story

From a family's passion to a neighborhood favorite.

A Tradition of Freshness

AA Sushi was born from a simple idea: to bring the authentic taste of Japanese sushi to Kirkland with a commitment to quality and freshness. Our founder, Chef Tanaka, grew up in a small coastal town in Japan, where the daily catch was a way of life. He brought those traditions with him, creating a menu that celebrates the pure, delicate flavors of the sea.

Every morning, we source the finest fish and ingredients, ensuring that each piece of sushi we serve is a testament to our dedication. It's not just about food for us; it's about sharing a piece of our heritage with every guest who walks through our doors.

We are more than just a restaurant; we are a family. We invite you to join us, share a meal, and become a part of our story.
